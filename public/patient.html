Frequently Asked Questions

Is my information kept confidential?

Yes, we take your privacy seriously. All your personal and health information is encrypted and securely stored. We never share your data without your explicit consent.

How do I book an online consultation?

To book an online consultation, log in to your account, go to the "Appointments" section, and choose a available time slot with your preferred healthcare provider.

Are the community forums moderated?

Yes, our community forums are moderated by trained professionals to ensure a safe, supportive, and informative environment for all users.
